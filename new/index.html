Jerry Can-Carry
Skills Developed: SolidWork, Design Concepts

Water transportation can be a really tough process for people in developing country. Could you immagine yourself walking for miles and miles with a 25-kilogram jerry can, filled with water? This Jerry Can-Carry is designed to help improve people's lives in developing country. Instead of carrying one heavy jerry can at time, they can know carry two of them at the same time! The Jerry Can-Carry will provide a support and stability so that they do not hurt their body while performing such intense activity.

This project involves sketching the design of the Jerry Can-Carry, creating QFD chart and user requirement specifications, building prototypes, testing the product then fianlly simulating the product with SolidWork.

Calculator Chaos
Play the Game!
Tools: HTML 5, CSS 3, Photoshop, JavaScript
Calculator Chaos was a project I made in a high school programming course. With some of the given codes, I had to design the interface and the fucntion of the game.
The objective in this game is too use a broken calculator to calculate given number, shown in a box besides the calculator, within a limited amount of time. The users will first have a choice to select the level they want to play. Once they click the button 'GO', the timer will start. When the timer stops, the game will show the final result whether they win or lose.
