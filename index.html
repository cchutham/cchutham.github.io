Jerry Can-Carry
Skills Developed: SolidWork, Design Concepts

Water transportation can be a really tough process for people in developing countries. Could you immagine yourself walking for miles and miles with a 25-kilogram jerry can, filled with water? This Jerry Can-Carry is designed to help improve people's lives in developing country. Instead of carrying one heavy jerry can at a time, they can now carry two of them at the same time! The Jerry Can-Carry will provide a support and stability so that they do not hurt their body while performing such physical intensive activity.

This project involves sketching the design of the Jerry Can-Carry, creating QFD chart and user requirement specifications, simulating the product with SolidWork, building prototypes, and testing the product.

Calculator Chaos
Play the Game!
Tools: HTML 5, CSS 3, Photoshop, JavaScript

Calculator Chaos was a project I made in a high school programming course. With some of the given codes, I had to design the interface and the fucntionality of the game.

The objective of the game is to use a broken calculator to calculate a given number, shown in a box besides the calculator, within a limited amount of time. Users will first have a choice to select the level they want to play. Once they click the 'GO' button, the timer will start. When the timer stops, the game will show the final result whether they win or lose.

Lemonade Stand
Tool: C++

Lemonade stand is a supply-demand forecasting console game. The main goal of the game is to sell lemonade, and meet the profit goal that the user has set in the beginning of the game.

The game begins by asking users to enter their name, number of days they want to sell lemonade, and amount of profit they want to make by the end of the game. Before selling lemonade, users are required to buy the ingredients to make lemonade. There will be market trend information for the user to determine if the price of the ingredients will go up or down as well as the weather forcast that will predict the sales of lemonade in that day.
