♠ Prom Queen Nails ♠

Materials: Sally Hansen Complete Salon Manicure Nail Color, Debut-Tint
Skin Food Pedicure Sparkle #7, Sparkle Sun Yellow
OPI Base Coat

Step 1: Apply the base coat on your nails first.
Step 2 : Use the sparkling pearl colored (Sally Hansen Complete Salon Manicure Nail Color, Debut-Tint) nail polish to apply as the base color of your nails. Let the nail polish dry before you move to the next step.
Step 3: Since this color looks a bit too light, try to repeat step 2 until you feel like the color is solid enough. After this, be patient and wait until the polish completely dry.
Step 4: Make sure that the polish on your nail is dry then start applying the golden nail polish(Skin Food PedicureSparkle #7, Sparkle Sun Yellow) on the tips of your nails. Try to do it slowly to make a thin layer and natural looking.

OR

Use the sponge to create the gradient on your nails. Simply get the golden nail polish on a make up sponge and gently dab it on your nails. *Tips: Try not to make it as a straight line, instead try to make it look spontaneous.
